About AWM Financial

Crawford Anderson is the founder of AWM Financial. A finance major at UNC Charlotte, he combines formal education with hands-on experience managing his own investment portfolios, building disciplined saving systems, and applying long-term financial strategy.

Crawford started AWM Financial with a simple goal: make money make sense. After seeing how little practical financial education exists for students and young adults, he set out to build a clear, accessible resource for the next generation.
